What We Do
MOUNTAIN WORK DONE RIGHT

From the ridgelines of Lookout Mountain to the valleys of Walker County — no slope is too steep, no terrain too tough. We bring the right equipment and real mountain know-how to every job.

▲

SITE GRADING

Precision rough and finish grading for new construction, pads, driveways, and parking lots. We read the terrain and work with the mountain — not against it.

⬟

EXCAVATION

Basement digs, foundation prep, utility trenching, and pond excavation. Heavy-grade Kubota equipment for rocky Appalachian soil and tight mountain lots.

🌲

LAND CLEARING

Timber removal, stump grinding, brush clearing, and debris hauling. We open up buildable land while preserving the trees and terrain you want to keep.

〰

DRAINAGE SOLUTIONS

French drains, swales, culverts, retention ponds, and grade-to-drain systems. Mountain water management is where most contractors fail — it’s where we start.

◼

ROAD & DRIVEWAY BUILDING

Private roads, access drives, logging roads, and subdivision infrastructure. Gravel base, proper crown, drainage ditching — roads that survive Appalachian weather.

⬡

DEMOLITION & HAULING

Structure demolition, concrete removal, site cleanup, and material hauling. We clear the old to make room for the new — and haul every piece of it out.
